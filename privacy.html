Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

PixlFix Underwater (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

PixlFix Underwater does not collect, store, or process any personal data directly. The App does not require user accounts, login, or submission of personal information.

2. Third-Party Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob mediation to display advertisements. AdMob and its mediated ad networks may automatically collect certain information to provide ads.

This information may include:

  • Device information (such as device model and operating system)
  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction and performance data

This data is collected and processed by Google and its advertising partners. PixlFix Underwater does not have access to or control over this data.

3. How Advertising Data Is Used

Advertising data collected by third-party services may be used to:

  • Serve personalized or non-personalized ads (depending on user consent)
  • Measure ad performance
  • Detect fraud and abuse

4. User Consent (GDPR & CCPA)

Where required by law (including the EU and UK), user consent for personalized advertising is obtained using Google’s consent mechanisms.

Users can manage or opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting their device settings or visiting:
https://adssettings.google.com

5. Children’s Privacy

PixlFix Underwater is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

Since PixlFix Underwater does not collect or store personal data, no user data is retained by the App itself.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
